Why is NO2 important?

  • NO2 is a pollutant itself
  • It also produces ozone (O3) which causes respiratory distress and harms plants

Vehicles

How is NO2 measured from space?

  • As sunlight passes through the atmosphere, NO2 absorbs some of it
  • The Aura satellite has an instrument (OMI) that measures that absorbance and derives NO2 column density from it

Data preprocessing
